Manual

This simple calculator can perform the following operations: addition, subtraction, division, multiplication, and exponentiation.

You will receive a message if the calculation result falls outside the range of ±9,007,199,254,740,991. Press any button to start again. Numbers smaller than 1×10-14 are rounded down to zero.

You can operate the calculator using a mouse, keyboard, number pad, or by touch if your device supports it. You can enter no more than 16 characters. The backspace, sign toggle and point buttons only apply to entered numbers, not the results of calculations.

Use the following symbols to perform the corresponding action:

"C" to clear
"Backspace" to delete
"~" to toggle the sign
"^" to exponentially increase
"/" to divide
"*" to multiply
"+" to add
"-" to subtract
"=" or "Enter" to calculate
